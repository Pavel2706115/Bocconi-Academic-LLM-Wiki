# FASB

## Overview

The Financial Accounting Standards Board (est. 1973) is the independent organization responsible for establishing and improving financial accounting and reporting standards (GAAP) in the United States.

## Key Facts

- Produces the **FASB Accounting Standards Codification** (official GAAP source)
- Operates under SEC oversight
- Board has 7 full-time members

## Relevance

- **Financial Accounting**: sets the rules for US financial reporting
- Counterpart to the IASB (international standards)
