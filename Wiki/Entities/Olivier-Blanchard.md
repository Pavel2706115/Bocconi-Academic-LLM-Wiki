# Olivier Blanchard

## Overview

Olivier Blanchard (b. 1948) is a French-American macroeconomist who served as Chief Economist of the IMF (2008-2015). His textbook *Macroeconomics: A European Perspective* (co-authored with Amighini and Giavazzi) is the primary reference for the Bocconi macroeconomics course.

## Key Contributions

- IS-LM-PC framework integrating goods, money, and labor markets
- Research on fiscal policy, public debt sustainability, and hysteresis in unemployment
- Influential policy contributions during the Global Financial Crisis

## Relevance

- **Macroeconomics**: core textbook author; IS-LM, Phillips Curve, expectations framework
