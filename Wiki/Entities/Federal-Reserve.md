# Federal Reserve

## Overview

The Federal Reserve System (est. 1913) is the central bank of the United States, responsible for monetary policy, banking supervision, and financial stability.

## Structure

- **Board of Governors**: 7 members appointed by the President (Washington, DC)
- **12 Regional Federal Reserve Banks**: implement policy locally
- **FOMC** (Federal Open Market Committee): sets the Federal Funds Rate target

## Key Functions

1. Conduct monetary policy (open market operations, interest rate setting)
2. Supervise and regulate banks
3. Maintain financial stability
4. Provide financial services (payments, clearing)
