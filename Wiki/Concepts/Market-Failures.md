# Market Failures

## Definition

Market failures occur when the free market fails to allocate resources efficiently. They provide the primary economic justification for government intervention.

## Four Questions of Public Finance

1. **When** should the government intervene? → Market failures
2. **How** should it intervene? → Taxes, subsidies, regulation, provision
3. **What are the effects** of the intervention? → Efficiency and equity consequences
4. **Why** do governments intervene the way they do? → Political economy

## Types of Market Failures

- **Externalities**: costs/benefits not reflected in market prices
- **Public goods**: non-rival, non-excludable goods underprovided by markets
- **Asymmetric information**: one party has more info (adverse selection, moral hazard)
- **Market power**: monopolies/oligopolies restrict output
