# Financial Statements for Managers

## Definition

Managers use financial statements not just for external reporting but as internal tools for performance evaluation, resource allocation, and strategic planning. This concept bridges management accounting with financial accounting.

## Key Uses

- **Performance evaluation**: comparing actual vs budgeted results
- **Resource allocation**: identifying profitable vs unprofitable segments
- **Investor communication**: explaining financial health to stakeholders
- **Credit analysis**: assessing ability to service debt
