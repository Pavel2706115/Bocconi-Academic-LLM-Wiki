# Elasticity

## Definition

Elasticity measures the responsiveness of one variable to changes in another. It is a unit-free measure expressed as a percentage change ratio.

## Types

- **Price elasticity of demand**: %ΔQ_d / %ΔP (typically negative)
- **Income elasticity**: %ΔQ_d / %ΔIncome (positive for normal goods, negative for inferior)
- **Cross-price elasticity**: %ΔQ_d of good A / %ΔP of good B (positive for substitutes, negative for complements)
- **Price elasticity of supply**: %ΔQ_s / %ΔP

## Classification

| |E| | Type | Revenue Effect of Price ↑ |
|-----|------|---------------------------|
| > 1 | Elastic | Revenue ↓ |
| = 1 | Unit elastic | Revenue unchanged |
| < 1 | Inelastic | Revenue ↑ |
