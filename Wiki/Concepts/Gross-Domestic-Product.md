# Gross Domestic Product

## Definition

GDP is the total market value of all final goods and services produced within a country in a given period. It is the primary measure of aggregate economic activity.

## Measurement Approaches

- **Expenditure**: GDP = C + I + G + (X − M)
- **Income**: sum of all incomes earned (wages, profits, rent, interest)
- **Production**: sum of value added across all sectors

## Key Distinctions

- **Nominal vs Real GDP**: real GDP adjusts for price changes using a base year
- **GDP per capita**: GDP / population — proxy for living standards
- **GDP growth rate**: percentage change in real GDP — defines expansions and recessions

## Limitations

- Excludes non-market production (household work, informal economy)
- Ignores income distribution
- Does not capture environmental degradation or quality of life
