# Income Statement

## Definition

The income statement (statement of earnings, P&L) reports a company's financial performance over a period by showing revenues minus expenses to arrive at net income.

## Structure

Revenue − Cost of Goods Sold = **Gross Profit**
Gross Profit − Operating Expenses = **Operating Income**
Operating Income ± Non-operating items − Income Tax = **Net Income**

## Key Components

- **Revenue**: cash and promises received from delivering goods/services
- **Expenses**: resources consumed to earn revenue (COGS, wages, rent, depreciation, taxes)
- **Net income**: the "bottom line" — primary measure of profitability
