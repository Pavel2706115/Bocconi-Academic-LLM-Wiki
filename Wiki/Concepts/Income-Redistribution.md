# Income Redistribution

## Definition

Income redistribution refers to government policies that transfer resources from higher-income to lower-income individuals to reduce inequality and ensure a minimum standard of living.

## Mechanisms

- **Progressive taxation**: higher earners pay proportionally more
- **Transfer programs**: cash transfers, food stamps, housing subsidies
- **In-kind transfers**: education, healthcare provided directly by government
- **Minimum wage**: floor on labor compensation

## Trade-offs

- Redistribution improves equity but may reduce efficiency (Okun's "leaky bucket")
- The extent of redistribution reflects social preferences and political processes
