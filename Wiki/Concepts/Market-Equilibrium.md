# Market Equilibrium

## Definition

Market equilibrium is the price-quantity pair where the quantity demanded equals the quantity supplied. At this point, there is no tendency for the price to change.

## Mechanism

- **Excess supply** (price too high): sellers lower prices → Q_s decreases, Q_d increases
- **Excess demand** (price too low): buyers bid prices up → Q_s increases, Q_d decreases

## Comparative Statics

Analysing how equilibrium changes when supply or demand shifts:

| Change | Effect on Price | Effect on Quantity |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| Demand ↑ | ↑ | ↑ |
| Demand ↓ | ↓ | ↓ |
| Supply ↑ | ↓ | ↑ |
| Supply ↓ | ↑ | ↓ |
| Both ↑ | Ambiguous | ↑ |
