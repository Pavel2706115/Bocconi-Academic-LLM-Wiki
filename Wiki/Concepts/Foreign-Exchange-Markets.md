# Foreign Exchange Markets

## Definition

Foreign exchange (FX) markets are where currencies are traded. Exchange rates — the price of one currency in terms of another — are determined by supply and demand for currencies.

## Exchange Rate Systems

- **Floating**: determined by market forces (e.g., USD, EUR, GBP)
- **Fixed (pegged)**: government maintains a target rate
- **Managed float**: central bank intervenes occasionally

## Determinants of Exchange Rates

- Interest rate differentials (interest rate parity)
- Inflation differentials (purchasing power parity)
- Trade balances and capital flows
- Expectations and speculation
