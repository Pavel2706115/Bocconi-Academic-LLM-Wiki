# Pension Systems

## Definition

Pension systems provide income to individuals after retirement. The design of pension systems involves fundamental trade-offs between intergenerational equity, fiscal sustainability, and individual incentives.

## Types

- **Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG)**: current workers' contributions pay current retirees. Sustainability depends on demographics (worker-to-retiree ratio).
- **Fully Funded**: each generation saves and invests for its own retirement. Returns depend on capital market performance.

## Design Challenges

- **Aging population**: declining birth rates and longer life expectancy strain PAYG systems
- **Retirement age**: raising it improves sustainability but affects labor markets
- **Replacement rate**: benefit level as % of pre-retirement income
