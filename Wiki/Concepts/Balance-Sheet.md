# Balance Sheet

## Definition

The balance sheet (statement of financial position) reports a company's financial position at a specific point in time, listing what the company owns (assets), what it owes (liabilities), and the residual claim of owners (stockholders' equity).

## The Accounting Equation

**Assets = Liabilities + Stockholders' Equity**

## Components

### Assets (by liquidity)
Cash → Short-term investments → Accounts receivable → Inventory → Prepaid expenses → Long-term investments → Equipment → Buildings → Land → Intangibles

### Liabilities (by maturity)
Accounts payable → Accrued expenses → Notes payable → Taxes payable → Unearned revenue → Bonds payable

### Stockholders' Equity
- **Common stock**: amounts invested by shareholders
- **Retained earnings**: accumulated profits not distributed as dividends
