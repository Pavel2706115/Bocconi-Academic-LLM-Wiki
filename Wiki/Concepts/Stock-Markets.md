# Stock Markets

## Definition

Stock markets are venues where equity securities (shares of ownership in corporations) are issued and traded. They provide capital for companies and investment opportunities for individuals.

## Market Types

- **Primary market**: new shares issued (IPOs, seasoned equity offerings)
- **Secondary market**: existing shares traded (NYSE, NASDAQ)
- **Exchanges** vs **dealer/OTC markets**

## Instruments

- **Common stock**: voting rights + residual cash flow claims
- **Preferred stock**: priority dividends, usually no voting
- **ADRs**: foreign stocks trading domestically

## Efficient Market Hypothesis (EMH)

- **Weak form**: prices reflect all past trading data
- **Semi-strong form**: prices reflect all publicly available information
- **Strong form**: prices reflect all information, including private
