Can stock market fluctuations be forecasted? Not most of them! Major movements in stock prices are unpredictable The stock market follows a random walk with some regularities 

What are the possible impacts of economic shocks/policies on the stock market? 

It depends on whether 

- 1) Shocks are EXPECTED or UNEXPECTED 

Already incorporated in the current value of stocks  the fundamental value does not change 

 the stock market does not react

- the fundamentals behind the stock change in an unexpected way 

- the stock market reacts
