## BALANCE OF PAYMENTS 

- A set of accounts that summarizes a countryвҖҷs transactions with the rest of the world 

- It is composed by three main accounts: 

   1. Current Account (CA): records payments to and from the rest of the world 

   2. Capital Account (KA): acquisition of non-financial assets by residents in the rest of the world, or by non-residents 

      - CA + KA = exposure of an economy to the rest of the world 

   3. Financial Account: net changes in assets 

      - FA = how it is financed
