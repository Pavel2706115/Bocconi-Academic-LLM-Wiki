## Consumer’s Problem 

Ø Consumer’s economic problem is to allocate limited funds to competing needs and desires to maximize his well being 

Ø - a Chooses among **consumption bundles** combination of various goods - given a certain budget and prices 

- Ø Trade off: Decision to consume more of one good is a decision to consume less of another 

## Consumer’s Problem 

ØWe look at the consumer decision problem: what is the bundle that **maximizes his personal well-being** , given his budget and prices? ØWe answer this question for every possible price of a good 

ØThis gives us the individual demand function for a good
