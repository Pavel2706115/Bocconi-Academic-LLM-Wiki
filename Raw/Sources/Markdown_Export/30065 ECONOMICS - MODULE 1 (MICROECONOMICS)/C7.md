## Summary: Properties of Budget line 

ØBudget line is the boundary that separates affordable bundles from all others ØSlope of budget line = -PX/PY ØX-intercept is M/PX; Y-intercept is M/PY ØChange in income shifts the line without changing its slope ØChange in the price of a good rotates the line
