## **Field Project FAQ: Product Management & Marketing Strategy**

### **The Basics**
**What is the core topic of this project?**
The project focuses on **Product Management**, specifically within the realm of brand and product strategy. 

**What are the primary objectives?**
1. To define a marketing strategy for a specific product and brand.
2. To develop a strategy that aligns with and achieves the company’s stated goals.

**How do we choose our project?**
Each team must select one project from two available alternatives provided by the company.

---
New dog food that: 
- Surpasses the health benefits that are required nowadays from the dog foods, with innovative nutrients that are based on natural products
- Introduces new, modern and fun way of packaging that specifically catches the eye of Gen Z
- In addition to being available as a standalone purchase, this product is offered via a subscription service featuring a pre-mixed combo of 20% wet and 80% dry dog food
---

### **Output & Deliverables**
**What are the requirements for the final presentation?**
* **Slide Deck:** 14–15 content slides (title slides and image-only slides do not count toward this limit).
* **Supporting Docs:** Any necessary attachments or appendices.
* **Presentation Time:** A strictly timed 6-minute final presentation.

**What should the structure of the output look like?**
Your proposal should follow this general flow:
1.  **Objective**
2.  **Analysis**
3.  **Proposal Details**
4.  **Feasibility**
