Ø TR=PxQ

Ø Thus, for a given price P,in perfect competition, to maximize profits a firm chooses a sales output Q to maximize revenues minus costs:

## Short run Finding Profit-Maximizing Sales Quantity for Price-Taking Firms 

Ø Step 1: **Quantity rule** . For a price-taking firm, _P_ = _MR_ for all quantities. Identify positive sales quantities where _P_ = _MC_ . If it is satisfied by more than one positive sale then determine with produces the highest profit. 

Ø Step 2: **Shut-down rule** . Check whether the most profitable positive sale quantity results in greater profit than shutting down. Alternative ways: 

Ø check whether revenues are greater than variable costs: PxQ*≥VC(Q*) Ø P ≥ AVC(Q*)
