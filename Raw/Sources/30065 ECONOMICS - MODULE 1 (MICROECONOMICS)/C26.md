# Econ 30065: Microeconomics Sara Negrelli Labor Supply

## Labor Supply

- Consumers buy goods and services - Many are also sellers (e.g., sell their work effort) - _**Labor supply**_ refers to the sale of a consumer’s time and effort to an employer - How to study labor supply:

- For every bad, there is a corresponding good, defined by the absence of the bad.

- If people regard hours of work as a bad, the corresponding good is _leisure_ . - The price of hours of leisure is **wage** - **To understand supply of labor => we study the demand for leisure!**
