## “ ” Affordable bundles: budget set

- A bundle of goods (X,Y) is affordable if: It costs less than the amount of money the consumer has to spend (income M):

_P X_ + _P Y_ ≤ _M X Y_ - This is the consumer’s _**budget constraint**_

- - The _budget set_ is the set containing all the affordable bundles

- The _budget line_ contains all the bundles that use all the income M:

= _P X_ + _P Y M X Y_

## Summary: Properties of Budget line

- Budget line is the boundary that separates affordable bundles from all others - Slope of budget line = -PX/PY - X-intercept is M/PX;
- Y-intercept is M/PY - Change in income shifts the line without changing its slope - Change in the price of a good rotates the line ^4lrwd1
