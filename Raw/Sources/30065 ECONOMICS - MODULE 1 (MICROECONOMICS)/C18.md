## Cournot quantity competition

- **Cournot model of oligopoly:** firms choose how much to produce (Q) simultaneously, and the price clears the market given the total quantity produced

- Assume homogeneous goods - To keep things simple we study a duopoly

## How to solve the game?

- Simultaneous moves game - Infinite set of actions - _Thus:_

- 1- find best response functions

2- see where they cross each other: that is NE

## Cournot competition vs Bertrand Model

- Important difference from equilibrium in Bertrand market: in Cournot, equilibrium price is always above marginal cost - Deadweight loss in Cournot!

## Deadweight Loss from Duopoly versus Monopoly

- Will the aggregate surplus be higher in the Bertrand and Cournot equilibrium compared to the monopoly outcome? - In Bertrand Model: DWL=0

- - In Cournot Model: DWL smaller than in Monopoly

- The deadweight loss of monopoly is larger because the monopoly price is further above marginal cost than is the oligopoly price
