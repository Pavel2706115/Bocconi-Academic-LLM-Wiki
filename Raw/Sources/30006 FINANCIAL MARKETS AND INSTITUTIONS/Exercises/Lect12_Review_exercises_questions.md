**Ex 6**: A stock is selling today for $55. A put option on this stock that expires today is selling for $6.

**Q**: What must be the exercise price of this option?

**A**: Since the option expires today, it must be that you make a zero profit if you exercise it: no arbitrage should be possible! (this like the story that future price is equal to the spot price at the delivery date) Profit from  buying a put and exercising it: _X-55-6_ Set this equal to zero yields _X=61_
